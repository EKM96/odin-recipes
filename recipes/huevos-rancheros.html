Huevos Rancheros

Description

A egg ranch tender, accompanied with a crispy synchronized ham and cheese and a spicy ranchera sauce. Enjoy the different textures of this dish.

Ingredients

  • 3 tomatoes, (for the sauce)
  • 3 serrano chiles, (for the sauce)
  • 1/4 onions, (for the sauce)
  • 1 clove garlic, (for the sauce)
  • 1 tablespoon vegetable oil, (for the sauce)
  • 1 tablespoon salt
  • 1/2 teaspoons pepper, (for the sauce)
  • 4 tablespoons vegetable oil
  • 8 corn tortillas
  • 8 eggs
  • 1/2 teaspoons salt
  • 1/4 teaspoons pepper
  • 1/2 tablespoons vegetable oil
  • 1 can beans, (440 g)

Steps

  1. In a comal over medium heat, pat the tomatoes, chilies, onion and garlic.
  2. Blend the ingredients until you get a homogeneous mixture.
  3. Heat the oil in a pot over medium heat, and add the sauce. Let it boil and fry for 5 minutes. Season and reserve.
  4. In a skillet over medium heat, heat the oil and fry the corn tortillas 1 minute. Drain on absorbent paper and reserve.
  5. In the same pan, fry the eggs to taste and season.
  6. Reheat the beans in a pan with oil. Reservation.
  7. Serve two tortillas in an extended dish, place two eggs on top and serve with ranchera sauce and beans.

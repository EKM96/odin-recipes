Chilaquiles Verdes

Description

Knowing how to prepare green chilaquiles is a must for any enthusiast of Mexican food! If you still don't know how to make them, fast and easy,

these green chilaquiles are here to save the day! Garnished with crema, cheese, and onion, these crunchy chilaquiles verdes will be ready in no time.

Ingredients

  • 8 tomatillos
  • 1/4 onions
  • 4 serrano chiles
  • 1 clove garlic
  • 1 bunch fresh cilantro
  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
  • tortillas, stale, cut into triangles
  • vegetable oil, for frying
  • 1 chicken breast, cooked and shredded
  • sour cream, for garnish
  • queso fresco, crumbled for garnish
  • onion, sliced

Steps

  1. For the green sauce: In a pot with boiling water, cook tomatillos, onion, and serrano chiles for 5 minutes.
  2. Transfer cooked ingredients, garlic, and cilantro to a blender and blend until smooth.
  3. In a skillet, add vegetable oil and fry the sauce. Cook for 5 minutes. Reserve.
  4. For the tortilla chips: Heat a deep skillet and add enough oil for frying. Fry tortilla chips for 3 minutes.
  5. For assembling the chilaquiles: Make a bed of tortilla chips topped with shredded chicken. Spoon hot green sauce over. Garnish with cream, cheese, and sliced onion.
